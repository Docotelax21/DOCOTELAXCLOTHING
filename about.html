About Docotela X

Docotela X began as a private experiment — a wayfinding through clothes that carry memory and motion. We believe in garments that breathe with a life beyond trend; they are vessels for a state of mind.

We make small runs. We work with hands. Each collection is a curated chapter — quiet, fierce, and made for a body that wants to move freely. When people asked us to conform, we doubled down on freedom. The result: a language of material and restraint.

We also cherish the people who walk with us: the artists, the makers, the friends and the collectors. That care shows up stitched into seams and signed into small runs. Docotela X exists for those who want to wear conviction, not fashion.
